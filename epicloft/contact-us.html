Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical loft conversion take?
Most standard loft conversions take between 6-8 weeks to complete. More complex projects might take 10-12 weeks. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do I need planning permission for a loft conversion?
Many loft conversions fall under permitted development rights, meaning they don't require planning permission. However, this depends on the type of conversion and your property. We'll advise you during our initial assessment and handle any necessary applications.
What is your payment schedule?
We typically work with a stage payment system - an initial deposit, followed by payments at key project milestones. This ensures you only pay for work that has been completed to your satisfaction. We'll outline the full schedule in your quote.
Is my loft suitable for conversion?
Most lofts are suitable for conversion. Key factors include the height of your loft (ideally at least 2.2m at the highest point), the roof structure, and available space. Our free assessment will quickly determine if your loft is suitable.
Do you provide guarantees on your work?
Yes, we provide a comprehensive 10-year guarantee on all our loft conversions, covering both structural work and finishing. We also provide manufacturer warranties for all installed products and materials.
Can I stay in my home during the conversion work?
Yes, most of our clients continue to live in their homes during the conversion process. We make every effort to minimize disruption to your daily life, working efficiently and keeping noise and mess to a minimum.
