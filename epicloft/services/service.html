Discover Our Range of Loft Conversion Services

We offer a comprehensive range of loft conversion solutions to transform your unused attic space into a functional and beautiful living area. Choose the perfect conversion type for your home.

Dormer Loft Conversion

One of the most popular types of loft conversions, dormers extend the existing roof space to create additional headroom and floor space. Perfect for adding extra bedrooms or home offices.

Learn More

Mansard Loft Conversion

Mansard conversions have a flat roof with a back wall sloping at a 72-degree angle, maximizing internal space. Ideal for properties where maximum space is needed.

Learn More

Roof Light Conversion

The simplest and most cost-effective option, roof light conversions add skylights to your existing roof without altering its structure. Perfect for homes with ample roof space.

Learn More

Hip-to-Gable Conversion

Ideal for detached or semi-detached houses with a hipped roof, this conversion straightens the sloping side to create a vertical wall. Great for maximizing space in properties with hipped roofs.

Learn More

Modular Loft Conversion

Modular conversions are pre-fabricated off-site and installed in days. Perfect for those seeking minimal disruption and a faster installation process.

Learn More
