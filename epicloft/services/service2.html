Key Advantages of Mansard Loft Conversion

Maximizes Space

Unlike other loft conversions, a Mansard conversion significantly increases usable floor space and head height, making it feel like a natural extension of your home.

Enhances Property Value

A Mansard loft conversion can add substantial value to your property, making it a worthwhile investment for homeowners in London.

Flexible Design

You can customize your Mansard loft to include bedrooms, bathrooms, home offices, or even an entertainment space with ease.

Types of Mansard Loft Conversions

Rear Mansard

The most common type, altering the rear slope of the roof to create a near-vertical wall. Ideal for Victorian and Georgian terraced homes.

  • ✓ Maximizes internal space
  • ✓ Enhances property value
  • ✓ Blends with traditional architecture

Double Mansard

Combines front and rear conversions, effectively adding a full additional floor to the home.

  • ✓ Largest increase in space
  • ✓ Ideal for multiple rooms
  • ✓ Great for large families

L-Shaped Mansard

Extends over an existing rear extension, creating an L-shape. Perfect for Victorian and Edwardian properties.

  • ✓ Multiple functional spaces
  • ✓ Optimizes house footprint
  • ✓ Maximizes floor area
