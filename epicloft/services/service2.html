Types of Mansard Loft Conversions

Rear Mansard

Most common type, altering the rear roof slope to create a near-vertical wall. Ideal for Victorian and Georgian terraced homes.

Double Mansard

Combines front and rear conversions, effectively adding a full additional floor. Perfect for townhouses and period properties.

L-Shaped Mansard

Extends over an existing rear extension, creating an L-shape. Great for optimizing house footprint.
