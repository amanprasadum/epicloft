Types of Loft Conversions

We specialize in different types of loft conversions to suit all home styles

Dormer Loft Conversion

A dormer conversion extends your existing roof vertically, creating additional headroom and floor space. Perfect for adding a spacious bedroom, office, or bathroom.

Read More

Mansard Loft Conversion

This type completely alters the roof's shape, creating almost a full additional storey. Ideal for homes in urban areas where maximizing space is crucial.

Read More

Roof Light Loft Conversion

The most cost-effective option, roof light conversions involve installing skylights without changing the roof structure. Perfect for homes with enough existing loft space.

Read More

Hip-to-Gable Loft Conversion

This type extends a hipped roof to form a vertical gable, creating extra usable space. Especially beneficial for semi-detached or end-of-terrace houses.

Read More

Modular Loft Conversions

A fast and modern alternative with pre-fabricated sections built off-site and installed quickly. Reduces on-site construction time and ensures high precision.

Read More
