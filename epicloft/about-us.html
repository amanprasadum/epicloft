Our Story

Founded in 2010, EPIC Loft Conversion has established itself as one of London's leading specialists in loft conversions and home transformations. We've built our reputation on delivering exceptional quality, innovative designs, and outstanding customer service.

What started as a small team of passionate builders has grown into a full-service loft conversion company with over 50 dedicated professionals, each bringing their unique expertise to every project we undertake.

Our mission is simple: to transform underutilized spaces into beautiful, functional living areas that enhance your home and lifestyle. Whether you're looking to create an extra bedroom, home office, or entertainment space, our team is committed to bringing your vision to life.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming London's most trusted loft conversion company, our journey has been one of continuous growth, learning, and excellence.

Throughout our history, we've maintained our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, adapting to new challenges and embracing innovative technologies to deliver the best possible results.

2010 - Foundation

EPIC Loft Conversion was founded by John Smith and Michael Brown, two experienced builders with a vision to create a company focused on quality loft conversions in London.

2013 - Expansion

After completing over 100 successful projects, we expanded our team to include architects, interior designers, and project managers, offering a more comprehensive service.

2016 - Award Recognition

Our dedication to excellence was recognized when we received the London Home Builder Award for "Best Loft Conversion Company," a testament to our team's hard work.

2019 - Innovation Focus

We introduced eco-friendly and energy-efficient conversion options, implementing sustainable practices and materials in our projects.

2022 - Digital Transformation

Embraced 3D modeling and virtual reality technology to help clients visualize their future loft spaces before construction begins.

Today

Now with over 850 successful projects completed, we continue to lead the industry with our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.
